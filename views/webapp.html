Additionally, AngularJS and Angular Material were also used to construct the front end.

If you access the website through a smartphone, you will be prompted to play the game through FB Messenger.

Note that opening the page in several tabs in your browser will effectively register a different player for each tab. This way, if no users are connected, opening 7 tabs will allow you to play the game.

Chat

All messages that you have received in the game are shown in the chat. Each player has a predetermined color that appears when they speak. Your messages are displayed to the right of the chat panel.

Game status messages are shown in red. They contain useful information about the different events that have happened during the game.

For you to send a message, type in the box at the end of the chat panel and press enter.

Status Bar

It consists of two modes: users and roles. You can change between the two modes by clicking on the blue tabs.

The leftmost image shows the users mode. During the voting phase and the night phase you can click on the alias of the player who you wish to target.

The rightmost image shows the roles mode. This mode displays the name of each role that is in the game.

Revealed roles show the alias and the avatar of the player that had the role. The roles that have not yet been revealed display an image of a masked avatar.
